Challenges

Fuel

In this challenge, players must navigate their vehicle efficiently through city streets while managing fuel consumption.
The goal is to cover the maximum distance possible without running out of fuel, emphasizing careful driving and strategic fuel management.

Challenges

Parking

In this challenge, players must navigate their vehicle into designated parking spots with precision and accuracy. The parking mode tests careful maneuvering and understanding of the car's dimensions.
The goal is to park correctly within the time limit, emphasizing patience, practice, and strategic planning. This mode enhances players' driving skills in the game's urban environment

Challenges

Broken Brake

In the Broken Brake mode, players must navigate their vehicle through city streets without the ability to use brakes. This challenge tests players' skills in controlling speed, avoiding collisions, and strategically using the car's momentum.
The goal is to drive as far as possible without crashing, emphasizing quick reflexes and careful planning. This mode offers a thrilling and intense driving experience, pushing players to their limits in managing a runaway vehicle

Challenges

Truck

In Truck Mode, players must navigate larger, more cumbersome vehicles through city streets and tight spaces. This challenge tests players' skills in handling the slower acceleration, wider turns, and greater size of trucks compared to regular cars.
The goal is to complete tasks like delivering goods or parking without causing accidents, emphasizing precision and careful planning. This mode offers a unique driving experience, enhancing players' abilities to manage different vehicle dynamics within the game.

Challenges

Lane

In Lane Mode, players navigate their vehicle within specific lanes, avoiding collisions and adapting to traffic conditions. The goal is to drive safely and efficiently while managing lane changes and obstacles, testing spatial awareness and precision.

Challenges

Highway

In Highway Mode, players drive at high speeds on expansive highways, focusing on lane management and overtaking other vehicles. The challenge is to maintain control and avoid collisions while navigating through fast-moving traffic.
The goal is to cover the longest distance safely and efficiently, testing speed management and driving skills
